Approximating implicit curves on plane and surface triangulations with affine arithmetic

F.C. Nascimento, A. Paiva, L.H. Figueiredo and J. Stolfi. Computers & Graphics 40, pp. 36-48, 2014.
We present a spatially and geometrically adaptive method for computing a robust polygonal approximation of an implicit curve defined on a planar region or on a triangulated surface. Our method uses affine arithmetic to identify regions where the curve lies inside a thin strip. Unlike other interval methods, even those based on affine arithmetic, our method works on both rectangular and triangular decompositions and can use any refinement scheme that the decomposition offers. 2012

Approximating implicit curves on triangulations with affine arithmetic

A. Paiva, F.C. Nascimento, L.H. Figueiredo and J. Stolfi. Proceedings of SIBGRAPI 2012, IEEE Press, pp. 94-101, 2012.
We present an adaptive method for computing a robust polygonal approximation of an implicit curve in the plane that uses affine arithmetic to identify regions where the curve lies inside a thin strip. Unlike other interval methods, even those based on affine arithmetic, our method works on triangulations, not only on rectangular quadtrees. [pdf]
